Cold Turkey

Acoustic Blues duo Cold Turkey will be accompanying the Saturday afternoon session.

Featuring:
Pete Reaney is a highly respected harmonica player and vocalist who has played on many sessions and gigged with a lot great artists. He appeared on Mark Lamars National Radio program as well as a few local stations.
Paul Reaney is a well travelled guitarist and vocalist and an award winning songwriter.
Both are seasoned performers and a good time will be had by all.
