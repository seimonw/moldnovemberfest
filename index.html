10th Novemberfest - 2022

Mold’s Novemberfest is a festival of Real Ale, Real Food and Real Entertainment! This year the tenth Novemberfest will take place from the 11th to 13th November 2022.

The main focal point is in St Mary’s Church Hall, hosting a great selection of real ales, craft beers, real lager, local cider and great wines, accompanied by great food and live music.

This event is organised by Mold Town Council with the support of the local CAMRA (Campaign for Real Ale) branch, and a group of volunteers. Novemberfest is a not-for-profit festival, all proceeds of the festival go towards putting on other events in the town and a nominated charitable cause.

Friday Evening

6:30 - 10:30pm

Fletch

Fletch are five friends from Chester who have been playing together in ‘Fletch’ for the past 10 years.

We’ve played hundreds of gigs to thousands of people and we love what we do! Our set lists are always high-energy and we guarantee to fill any dance floor you put in front of us! We specialise in weddings but we also play lots of corporate events too. Whatever the occasion, our only mission is to get you dancing and put a smile on your face!

You may have enjoyed them recently at Mold Food Festival.

Saturday Afternoon

12:30 - 5:30pm

Ramshackle

Acoustic Blues collaboration Ramshackle will be accompanying the Saturday afternoon session.

RAMSHACKLE is an acoustic based collaboration of musicians from three North Wales and Cheshire bands, with many years of performing experience between them.
From Chester's Blueswagon and Deportees bands we have Frank Neil and Adrian Dear on vocals, harmonica and guitar, while music fans from Mold may recognise David Clutton, Tim Holdsworth and Dave Clark from Wrexham's Jacobs Ladder, regular performers at pub music nights in town, bringing more vocals, guitar, bass and percussion to the party.
They will be playing a selection of blues, pop and rock classics, in a laid back acoustic style, with the emphasis on a good time being had by all!

Saturday Evening

6:30 - 10:30pm

My Eleventh Toe

As a live covers band, MYELEVENTHTOE pride themselves on delivering the very best show they can with cover songs packed full of hits and nostalgia from the very best bands and artists. David, Tom, Emlyn and Duncan make sure they deliver a big powerful live show.
