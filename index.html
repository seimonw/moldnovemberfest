Novemberfest - 2025

Mold’s Novemberfest is a festival of Real Ale, Real Food and Real Entertainment! This year the thirteenth Novemberfest will take place on the 14th and 15th November 2025.

The main focal point is in The Pod (formerly St Mary’s Church Hall), hosting a great selection of real ales, craft beers, real lager, local cider and great wines, accompanied by great food and live music.

This event is organised by Mold Town Council with the support of the local CAMRA (Campaign for Real Ale) branch, and a group of volunteers. Novemberfest is a not-for-profit festival, all proceeds of the festival go towards putting on other events in the town and a nominated charitable cause.
