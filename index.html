Novemberfest - 2024

Mold’s Novemberfest is a festival of Real Ale, Real Food and Real Entertainment! This year the twelfth Novemberfest will take place on the 8th and 9th November 2024.

The main focal point is in The Pod (formerly St Mary’s Church Hall), hosting a great selection of real ales, craft beers, real lager, local cider and great wines, accompanied by great food and live music.

This event is organised by Mold Town Council with the support of the local CAMRA (Campaign for Real Ale) branch, and a group of volunteers. Novemberfest is a not-for-profit festival, all proceeds of the festival go towards putting on other events in the town and a nominated charitable cause.

Friday Evening

6:00 - 10:30pm

The C90s

The C90s were formed soon after lockdown, When Mark, placed an ad online looking for like minded musicians, to form a band that didn't play, sweet home Allabama or whiskey in the jar. After a few changes they settled on its current lineup of Amanda Holmes, vocals, Simon Alexander, guitar, Mark Gunning Bass guitar, Mark 'Bonus' Jones, Drums and Tony Warren, Keyboard. The band tries to choose interesting songs whilst picking songs that people remember, but not the songs you'd initially think. From Alanis Morrisette, to Basement Jaxx, Jamiroquai to Pearl jam, there is something for everyone, from all the popular musical movements of the last great decade of music.
